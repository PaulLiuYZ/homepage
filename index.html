Short Bio
Yuzhou Liu is pusuring his master degree (Academic master, Control Science and Engineering, Pattern recognition and intelligent system) in the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences since September, 2021 Prior to that, he received the B.S. degree in Measurement and Control Technology and Instruments from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2021. His research interests lie in the area of computer vision, specifically focusing on 3D reconstruction including structure from motion, SLAM and so on.
